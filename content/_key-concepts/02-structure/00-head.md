---

Articles follow the natural alphabetic ordering of files and folders. 
The following example demonstrates how to order articles and categories:

```
  ./
  ├── 00-head.md
  ├── 01-sub.md
  ├── 02-sub.md
  ├── 03-category/
  │   └── 01-subcat.md
  ├── 04-sub.md
  └── 05-tail.md
```
